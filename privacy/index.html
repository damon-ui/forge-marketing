By using FORGE, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

FORGE Travel Tools is a software platform that helps travel advisors create trip comparisons and itineraries for their clients. We're based in Irmo, South Carolina.

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at privacy@forgetravel.co.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use FORGE, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, phone number, agency name when you create an account
  • Trip Data: Trip details, pricing, dates, vendor information, and other content you enter or upload
  • Client Information: Names and email addresses of clients you create comparisons for
  • Uploaded Content: Screenshots, PDFs, and documents you upload for AI extraction
  • Payment Information: Billing details when you subscribe (processed by Stripe—we don't store your full card number)
  • Communications: Messages you send us through support or feedback channels

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Service, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Features you use, pages you visit, actions you take
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, referring pages
  • Cookies: We use cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences

2.3 Information from Clients

When your clients view comparisons or vote on options, we collect:

  • Their votes and feedback on trip options
  • Basic device/browser information
  • IP address for security purposes

We don't require clients to create accounts or provide personal information beyond what's needed for voting.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Purpose What This Means
Provide the Service Store your trips, generate comparisons, process AI extractions, deliver itineraries to your clients
Improve the Service Understand how features are used, fix bugs, develop new functionality
Communicate with You Send account notifications, respond to support requests, share important updates
Process Payments Handle subscription billing through our payment provider
Ensure Security Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
Comply with Law Meet legal obligations, respond to lawful requests

We don't sell your personal information. We don't use your trip data or client information for advertising purposes.

4. Information Sharing

4.1 When You Share Information

When you create a comparison and share the link, that information becomes visible to anyone with the link. You control what information you include and who you share links with.

4.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the Service (see Section 5 for details). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and only use it to provide services to us.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

4.4 Business Transfers

If we're involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We'll notify you of any change in ownership or use of your personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate FORGE:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Database and authentication All account and trip data
Cloudflare Hosting, CDN, security Traffic data, IP addresses
Anthropic (Claude) AI trip extraction Uploaded screenshots/PDFs for processing
Stripe Payment processing Billing information
Unsplash Hero images Search queries only

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them if you have concerns about how they handle data.

5.1 AI Processing

When you upload screenshots or documents for AI extraction, those images are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. Anthropic's data policies (available at https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy) apply to this processing. We don't use your uploaded content to train AI models—it's processed solely to extract trip information for your use. Uploaded screenshots and PDFs are stored for 120 days to allow you to review and re-extract information if needed, then automatically deleted.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Where Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on servers in the United States, operated by Supabase and Cloudflare. Both providers maintain SOC 2 compliance and industry-standard security practices.

6.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Secure authentication (magic links, password hashing)
  • Row-level security policies to isolate user data
  • Regular security reviews

6.3 No System Is Perfect

While we work hard to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Export

You can access and export your data from within the Service. If you need assistance, contact us.

7.2 Correction

You can update your account information and trip data directly in the Service.

7.3 Deletion

You can delete individual trips, comparisons, or your entire account. When you delete your account, we'll remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we need to retain it for legal purposes.

7.4 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing message. You'll still receive transactional emails about your account.

7.5 Cookies

Most browsers let you control cookies through settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use the Service (for example, staying logged in).

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

Data Type Retention Period
Account information Until you delete your account
Trip data and comparisons Until you delete them or your account
Uploaded images for AI extraction 120 days, then automatically deleted
Usage logs 90 days
Payment records 7 years (legal requirement)
Support communications 2 years after resolution

After account deletion, we may retain anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

9. International Users

FORGE is operated from the United States. If you're using the Service from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

FORGE is designed for and marketed to US-based travel advisors. If you access the Service from outside the US, you acknowledge that your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States under US law.

10. Children's Privacy

FORGE is designed for adult travel advisors. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we'll delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we'll notify you by email or through the Service. The "Effective Date" at the top shows when this policy was last updated.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the updated policy.
