Frequently Asked Questions

Who is FORGE for?

Travel advisors who want professional tools without enterprise pricing. Whether you're part-time, just starting out, or a seasoned pro—FORGE was built specifically for advisors who don't have hours to spend on data entry. We built it because we needed it ourselves.

How does the AI ingestion work?

Paste or upload a screenshot, PDF, or booking confirmation. FORGE uses AI to extract the key details (dates, prices, cabin types, hotel info, etc.) and builds a structured trip you can edit and refine. Or just chat with FORGE—describe the trip in your own words and let AI build it for you.

What's a "comparison"?

When you're ready to share options with a client, you create a comparison. Clients see a side-by-side view and can vote on their favorite. It's how you go from "here are some options" to "let's book this one."

Can I try it before paying?

Yes. We'll offer a free trial at launch so you can see how it fits your workflow.

What makes FORGE different from other tools?

Three things: First, AI-first data entry—while other tools have you dragging and dropping or typing manually, FORGE extracts details from screenshots and PDFs automatically. Second, client voting—your clients can vote on their favorite options right from the comparison, so you know exactly what resonates. Third, price—at $15/month, we're built for advisors who book 5-50 trips a year, not agencies with dedicated ops teams.
