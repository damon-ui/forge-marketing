Frequently Asked Questions

Who is FORGE for?

Travel advisors who want professional tools without enterprise pricing. Whether you're part-time, just starting out, or a seasoned pro who's tired of overpaying—FORGE is built for you.

How does the AI ingestion work?

Paste or upload a screenshot, PDF, or booking confirmation. FORGE uses AI to extract the key details (dates, prices, cabin types, hotel info, etc.) and builds a structured trip you can edit and refine.

What's a "comparison"?

When you're ready to share options with a client, you create a comparison. Clients see a side-by-side view and can vote on their favorite. It's how you go from "here are some options" to "let's book this one."

Can I try it before paying?

Yes. We'll offer a free trial at launch so you can see how it fits your workflow.

What makes FORGE different from other tools?

Most tools were built for large agencies with full-time staff. FORGE is built for real advisors—people who work nights and weekends building something meaningful. We combine AI-powered speed, beautiful client-facing tools, and pricing that respects your budget.
