Frequently Asked Questions

Who is FORGE for?

Travel advisors who want professional tools without enterprise pricing. Whether you're part-time, just starting out, or a seasoned pro—FORGE was built specifically for advisors who don't have hours to spend on data entry. We built it because we needed it ourselves.

How does the AI ingestion work?

Paste or upload a screenshot, PDF, or booking confirmation. FORGE uses AI to extract the key details (dates, prices, cabin types, hotel info, etc.) and builds a structured trip you can edit and refine. Or just chat with FORGE—describe the trip in your own words and let AI build it for you.

What's a "comparison"?

When you're ready to share options with a client, you create a comparison. Clients see a side-by-side view and can vote on their favorite. It's how you go from "here are some options" to "let's book this one."

Can I try it before paying?

Yes! Every account starts with a 14-day free trial. You get full access to all features—build trips, set up your CRM, customize your branding. The only limitation is you can't share comparisons or itineraries with clients until you subscribe. That way you can explore everything risk-free.

What's the difference between Founder and Standard?

Nothing feature-wise—both get everything. The only difference is price. Founder pricing ($15/month) is locked forever for the first 100 advisors. After those spots fill, new signups pay Standard pricing ($18/month). If you're on the fence, grab a Founder spot while they last.

What makes FORGE different from other tools?

One connected workflow. Most tools are a collection of separate features—you build here, invoice there, track clients somewhere else. FORGE connects everything: client intake flows to your CRM, which flows to trip building, which flows to itinerary. Every tool knows about every client. Zero app-hopping, zero re-entering data.
